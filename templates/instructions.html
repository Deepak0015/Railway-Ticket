Season Pass

1

Select Commute Route

Choose your regular commuting stations. Season passes are valid for unlimited travel between these stations.

2

Choose Start Date

Select when you want your pass to activate (between tomorrow and 5 days from now). The pass will be valid for 30 days.

3

Complete Application

Provide your details, take a verification photo, and complete payment to activate your pass.

Photo Verification

All bookings require a photo for identity verification. Click the camera icon to take your picture in a well-lit area.

Fare Calculation

Fares are automatically calculated based on distance. Season passes offer 30 days of unlimited travel for a fixed price.

Mobile Tickets

All tickets are available digitally. You can access them anytime from your Bookings page.
